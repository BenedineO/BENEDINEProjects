Creating my Visualization Plot.

Creating a visualization using scatterplot to show the relationship between the flipper length and body mass of the 3 penguins species analyzed in the dataset, and storing the output in a new variable. I used the labs() to include title, subtitle and caption to the plot.

new_penguins <- ggplot(data = penguins) +
geom_point(mapping = aes(x = flipper_length_mm, y = body_mass_g, color=species))+
  labs(title= "Palmer Penguins: Body Mass Vs. Flipper Length", subtitle = "Sample of Three Species", caption ="Data collected by Dr. Kristen Gorman")
  • Adding text to my visual using the annotate().
new_penguins + annotate("text", x=220, y=3700, label= "The Gentoos are the largest",color="royalblue", angle=15)
